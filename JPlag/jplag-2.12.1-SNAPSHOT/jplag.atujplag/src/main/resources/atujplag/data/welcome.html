Welcome to ATUJPLAG

This tool is especially designed to use the JPLAG web service .
You can find more information about JPLAG on www.jplag.de

Quickstart

To submit new files to JPLAG , just click  on new Submission in the toolbar.
To load informations about all submissions availables on the JPLAG server ,
go to the menubar,click on Extra and then on
To delete a submission ,go to the menu,click on the title of the submission
to be deleted and then go to the toolbar and click on Delete submission
To delete a submission that is availlable on the JPLAG server,first load
information about all submissions availlable on the JPLAG server,then
delete the submission as described above.

Warning

 You cannot store more than submissions on  the JPLAG server. 
 In more , a submission result cannot be stored more than 7 days.
 To avoid any error , please delete submissions when result have 
 been downloaded.
